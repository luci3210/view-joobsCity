Overseas Recruitment & Deployment Management System

To digitalize and centralize the entire overseas employment process - from applicant registration to deployment.

Innovation
Excellence
Collaboration

Most recruitment agencies still handle applications manually or with incomplete digital systems

  • Lost applicant records
  • Delays in document verification and deployment
  • Lack of real-time tracking (visa, contract, etc.)
  • Poor communication between agency, employer, and applicant

A web-based ORDMS that automates and tracks the entire process.

Applicant Management

Online registration (select job category)

Upload resume, passport, certificates

Application tracking (status updates: Screening → Interview → Medical → Visa → Deployed)

Automated notifications via email/SMS

Agency Dashboard

Manage multiple job categories
Assign recruiters or interviewers
Track candidate progress
Generate POEA/DMW reports

Employer Portal

Post job orders
View shortlisted candidates
Schedule interviews
Send job offers

Visa & Deployment Tracking

Track visa application status (submitted, approved, released)
Store flight info, ticket, and deployment date
Monitor deployed workers

Payment & Processing

Log agency fees, placement fees, and expenses
Print official receipts and reports

Reports & Analytics

Applicants per job type / destination
Deployment rate by month or region
Performance reports for agency branches
